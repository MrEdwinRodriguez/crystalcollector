Rules:

You will be given a random number to start the game.

There are four superheroes below. Each superhereo will add a specific amount of points to your total score when you click on them.

You win the game by matching your total to the random number. You lose if your total points goes over the random number.

The value of each superhereo is unkown until your click on it.

Each time that the game starts, the superhereo's values will change.
